Our achievements


Thailand's dramatic economic growth has caused numerous environmental issues. The country faces problems with air, declining wildlife populations, deforestation, soil erosion, water scarcity, and waste issues. As such, Thailand's economic growth has come at great cost in damage to its people and environment. The environmental problems have risen along with economic growth and urbanization. All of these problems have affected the quality of life and have added greater economic costs.

We, along with the government have checked for all such climate changes. We have spread awarness among all sections of the society regarding the pollution and environmental imbalance. Our company has also taken steps to reduce these prevailing problems and has also plantes ten thousand trees last year to help in sustaining the environmental balance.
