Our achievements


The Egypt of today is a complex social and political entity, and one that faces a struggle against poverty, population growth and authoritarian government on the road to equitable human development. It is one of the countries in the region where malnutrition is a challenge and is also among the bottom nine countries in the world for literacy

Our company has tried it's best to resolve these issues in the recent years. We've served the nation with food and other basic ammenities which the common mass have been deprived of. Campaigns for illeteracy have been organised and approx 10% of the then population have been made literate.
