Our achievements


Nepal is one of the underdeveloped countries of the world. It is facing a large number of social problems such as caste system, child labor, illiteracy, gender inequality, superstitions, religious conflicts and much more. 27% of the population is under absolute poverty. Because of poor growth in the agricultural sector, living standards in rural areas causes poverty.

In order to resolve these issues our company had taken several steps in each of the above listed domain. For poverty we have spread awarness among people such that they can make the best utilization of the locally available resources to earn their live hood and live a quality of life. For unemployment we have provided money for enhancing entrepreneurship and establishment of small, medium and large scaled industries to provide employment opportunities to all based on their skills. For issues like drug addiction, alcoholism etc. we've organised several programmes and campaigns where we have not made the youth but also their parent, aware of the negative influence of such things in human's life.
