Our achievements


In Turkey, racism and ethnic discrimination are present in its society and throughout its history, including institutional racism against non-Muslim and non-Sunni minorities. This appears mainly in the form of negative attitudes and actions by Turks towards people who are not considered ethnically Turkic.

Our organisation has helped many such people with clothing, jobs, food and other essentials. We along with WHO have also taken care of the health of common people in remote areas.
