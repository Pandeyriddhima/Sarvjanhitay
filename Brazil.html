Our achievements


Poverty in Brazil is most visually represented by the favelas, slums in the country's metropolitan areas and remote upcountry regions that suffer with economic underdevelopment and below-par standards of living. In Rio de Janeiro, about a fifth of its population of six million live in several hundred favelas, situated on steep, neglected land largely beyond the control and services of city authorities.

Our organisation has provided food and other necessities to the majority of people in Brazil, lying below the poverty line. We have also organised campaigns for vaccination to the people and have also made them aware of the employment opportunities available for them. Not only this but our company had also provided jobs to many such people there.
